# Osk 1.0.0 Next Steps

**Date:** 2026-03-27  
**Decision:** Address critical gaps before 1.0.0 release

---

## Summary

After capability assessment, we've identified three critical gaps that must be resolved before shipping 1.0.0:

1. **Evidence artifacts** - Export must contain actual audio/video, not just metadata
2. **Sensor validation** - Must validate 5-10 concurrent sensor streams  
3. **Semantic synthesis** - Must use AI (Ollama), not keyword matching

**Timeline:** 2-3 weeks  
**Risk:** Medium (synthesis has unknowns)

---

## Decision Points

### Option A: Proceed with Gap Remediation (RECOMMENDED)

Implement the three critical gaps before 1.0.0.

**Pros:**
- Product delivers on design intent
- Truthful marketing claims
- Solid foundation for future releases

**Cons:**
- 2-3 week delay
- Semantic synthesis may be challenging

**Plan:** See [`1.0.0-gap-remediation-plan.md`](./1.0.0-gap-remediation-plan.md)

### Option B: Ship 1.0.0 with Documented Limitations

Ship current state with explicit documentation of gaps.

**Pros:**
- Ship immediately
- Iterate in 1.1.0

**Cons:**
- Product doesn't match design intent
- Risk of user disappointment
- Harder to market effectively

**Not recommended** - The gaps are significant enough to misrepresent the product.

### Option C: Narrow Scope

De-scope sensor/synthesis from 1.0.0, focus on manual reporting only.

**Pros:**
- Ship faster
- Truthful about capabilities

**Cons:**
- Major feature cut
- Requires rebranding as "coordination tool" not "intelligence platform"

**Viable if timeline is critical** - But would need significant messaging changes.
